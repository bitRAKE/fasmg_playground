Each movement in the horizontal is a zero.
Each movement in the vertical is a one.

1st binary# in this series:
	0000000000000000000011111111111111111111
last:
	1111111111111111111100000000000000000000

For the numbers in between, the amount of zeros
should be the same as ones. In other words, the
ones and zeros have to be rearranged.

The total is:	40!/(20!)(20!) = 137846528820
